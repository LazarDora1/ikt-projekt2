STORY

At Christmas, we held the family gathering at our grandparents' house. We planned to exchange gifts in the evening, and so we did. Everyone received their presents one by one, but when it was my turn, it turned out that my gift – which was over 2 meters wide and 1.5 meters tall – had been left at home.
